Under Construction. Custom lakefront single story home by KHP Builders in the gated golf community of Bella Collina. Estimated for completion in Spring 2023. From the moment you enter you are welcomed by spectacular lake views and luxury finishes at every turn. The foyer is adorned with a built-in wine cellar leading to the great room with a 21 ft vaulted ceiling overlooking the expansive views of Lake Apopka. Large format tile, engineered hardwood flooring, recessed LED lighting and ceiling fans throughout. The fabulous gourmet kitchen with 14 ft coffered ceiling features Adornus Euro-Style wood frameless cabinetry, Signature Kitchen Suite stainless appliances with 48 inch dual gas and induction range top, built in dual combo-oven, 30 inch refrigerator column and 24 inch freezer column. Additional features include a Theramador built in coffee maker, Kraus 57 inch Stainless gourmet workstation sink and Waterdrop Tankless Reverse Osmosis system with Brizo reverse osmosis faucet. Master bedroom is completed with engineered hardwood flooring along with his and hers closets and a stacked washer/dryer. Master bathroom boasts Axent Primus Tankless floating toilet, dual vanities, LED mirrors and Kohler Thermostatic Shower System with body jets, rain head and handheld shower. Relax in the freestanding tub with deck-mount faucet. Laundry room features a 9 cb ft gas dryer with pedestal, 5.2 Cu ft washer and LG 18 inch Styler Steam Clothing Care System. Dedicated office, media/game room and bonus room are sure to please. The covered lanai is perfect for entertaining family and friends with an outdoor kitchen overlooking the extended pool patio and infinity edge 12 x 42 ft pool with cabana. Green home certifications include FGBC Green Home Certification, EPA Energy Star, EPA Indoor Plus, DOE Zero Energy Ready Home ( ZERH) Florida Water Star. Home offers solar panels and back up battery, Energy efficient SIP & LGS home framing with 16 + seer variable speed HVAC system and Panasonic ERV balanced ventilation system. Control 4 home automation for audio and video system. Garages offer oversized 18 x 8 with epoxy flooring and battery backup. This home has the opportunity for a private boat dock on Lake Apopka along with access to the community boat ramp on Lake Siena. Enjoy the many amenities that Bella Collina has to offer including an 18 hole Championship golf course designed by Sir Nick Faldo, Har-Tru tennis courts, outdoor pool complex, fitness center, pickle ball, free fitness classes, full service spa and multiple dining venues Call today to learn about the developer incentive!
